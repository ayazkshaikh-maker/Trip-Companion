City of Atlanta — APD VFH Section
Atlanta Taxi Regulations
Chapter 162, Atlanta City Code · GA HB 225
License & Permit Requirements
01
For-Hire Endorsement — State of Georgia issues endorsements through DDS. Required on your CDL/driver's license. License plate starts with "TX" for taxis.
02
CPNC — Certificate of Public Necessity and Convenience required. Annual renewal through Atlanta PD VFH Section.
03
Master Taxicab License — $1,500/year for a single cab. Required under GA Code § 40-2-168.
04
Georgia Residency — Must be GA resident for at least 1 year prior to application (Atlanta City Ordinance § 162-77).
05
Background Check — Fingerprinting by APD required. Cannot have 2+ Atlanta City Code violations in prior 2 years.
On-Road Rules — Must Know
06
Credit Cards Mandatory at ATL — All Vehicles for Hire at Hartsfield-Jackson MUST accept credit cards. Violation = impoundment.
07
Vehicle Age — Must be within the last 16 model years. Annual safety inspection through Atlanta PD.
08
Cleanliness — Dirty cab at taxi stand = correction sheet + forced to leave stand. VFH officers conduct spot inspections.
09
Written Receipts — Must issue written receipts to passengers on request.
10
Sales Tax — Register with Georgia DOR to collect and remit sales tax. Consult a tax professional.
